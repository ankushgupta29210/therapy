Do you offer direct billing?

+

Yes, we offer direct billing to most major extended health insurance providers including Pacific Blue Cross, Sun Life, Canada Life, Green Shield, and more. Please bring your insurance card to your first visit.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We require 24 hours notice for any cancellations or rescheduled appointments. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice will be subject to a cancellation fee equivalent to the cost of the session.

What should I wear to my appointment?

+

Please wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows the therapist to easily access the area being treated. Shorts and a tank top or t-shirt are generally recommended.
