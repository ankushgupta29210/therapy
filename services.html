Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of treatments tailored to your specific needs. From manual therapy to specialized activity-based assessments, our goal is to restore efficient movement, strength, and confidence.

01

Physiotherapy

Manual Therapy ∘ Exercise Prescription

Our core service focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal injuries. We combine hands-on manual therapy with individualized exercise programs to relieve pain, improve mobility, and help you return to the activities you love.

02

Specialties

IMS / Dry Needling ∘ Shockwave Therapy ∘ Vestibular

Targeted treatments for complex or stubborn issues. We utilize evidence-based modalities like Intramuscular Stimulation (IMS) to release tight muscle bands, and Shockwave Therapy to accelerate healing in chronic tendon injuries.

03

Activity Based

Clinical Pilates ∘ Running Assessment

Optimize your performance and prevent future injuries. We analyze your movement mechanics during specific activities to identify inefficiencies and provide targeted corrections to keep you moving at your best.
